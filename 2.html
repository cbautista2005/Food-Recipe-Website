⋆ Sinigang na Baboy is a Filipino pork dish simmered in a tangy tamarind broth with vegetables, often served with rice. ⋆

  • Home

☆ Ingredients:

  • 500g pork ribs or belly
  • 1 liter water
  • 1 tomato (quartered)
  • 1 onion (quartered)
  • 1 packet tamarind soup mix (or fresh tamarind)
  • 1 cup kangkong (water spinach)
  • 1 cup radish (sliced)
  • 1 cup sitaw (string beans)Salt and fish sauce to taste

☆ Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Boil pork in water with tomatoes and onions until tender.
  2. Step 2: Add tamarind mix (or mashed fresh tamarind) and stir.
  3. Step 3: Add radish and sitaw, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Add kangkong last, season with salt or fish sauce.

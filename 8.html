⋆ Beef Bulalo is a Filipino soup made with beef shank and bone marrow, simmered until tender in a savory broth with vegetables like corn, cabbage, and potatoes. ⋆

☆ Ingredients:

  • 8 cups water
  • 1/2 kg beef shank, cut to serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 pcs Knorr Beef Cubes
  • 1 pc sweet/japenese corn, cut into 3
  • 2 -3 pcs saba, sliced diagonally into 2
  • 1 cup baguio beans, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 pc Pak-Choi/Pechay Baguio, sliced To taste black pepper

☆ Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Boil water in a large pot with beef shanks and onions. Simmer for 1.5 hours, removing scum occasionally.
  2. Step 2: Add Knorr Beef Cubes, corn, and sliced saba.
  3. Step 3: Mix in Baguio beans and pechay, then simmer for 1 minute.
  4. Step 4: Season with pepper and your dish is ready!

⋆ Bicol Express is a Filipino dish made with pork cooked in coconut milk, shrimp paste, and chili peppers, creating a spicy and creamy flavor. ⋆

☆ Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly (sliced)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 4-6 red chilies (sliced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

☆ Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Sauté garlic, onion, and shrimp paste in a pan.
  2. Step 2: Add pork belly and cook until browned.
  3. Step 3: Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Add chilies and coconut cream, then cook until thick.
  5. Step 5: Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.

⋆ Kaldereta is a Filipino stew made with tender meat, usually beef, slow-cooked in a tomato-based sauce with potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. ⋆

☆ Ingredients:

  • 1 kg. beef brisket
  • ¼ pc. onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 2 Tbsp. liver spread
  • 1 (45g) pack Knorr Complete Recipe Mix Kaldereta dissolved in 2 1/2cups water
  • 1 pc. red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper diced
  • 1 piece potato diced
  • 1 pc carrot quartered
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 piece siling labuyo

☆ Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Fry beef in a hot pot with a little oil. Set aside.
  2. Step 2: Sauté garlic and onions in the same pot.
  3. Step 3: Return beef to the pot, add water, and bring to a boil. Simmer until the meat is tender.
  4. Step 4: Mix in liver spread and Knorr Complete Recipe Matrix Kaldereta (mixed with water). Boil for 2 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Add vegetables and simmer until the sauce thickens.
  6. Step 6: Serve and enjoy your Kaldereta!

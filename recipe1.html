Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish made with chicken marinated in a savory mix of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, then simmered until tender and flavorful.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Vinegar
  • 5 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 1 tsp Peppercorns
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, marinate chicken with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the marinated chicken (including the marinade) and cook for 15 minutes, allowing the chicken to brown slightly.
  4. Pour in water and simmer until the chicken is fully cooked and tender, about 25-30 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.

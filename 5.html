⋆ Lechon is a Filipino dish of whole roasted pig, cooked until the skin is crispy and the meat tender, often served during special occasions. ⋆

☆ Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly (skin-on)
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 stalks lemongrass (chopped)

☆ Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Rub the pork with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and vinegar.
  2. Step 2: Place lemongrass inside the rolled pork belly and tie with string.
  3. Step 3: Roast in an oven at 180°C (350°F) for 2-3 hours, turning occasionally.
  4. Step 4: Increase heat to 220°C (425°F) for 15 minutes to crisp the skin.Let rest before slicing.

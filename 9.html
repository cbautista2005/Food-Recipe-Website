⋆ Pork Afritada is a Filipino stew made with pork, cooked in a tomato-based sauce with potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, and peas. ⋆

☆ Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 pc medium sized onion, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 kg pork kasim, sliced into 2" cubes
  • 250 g (1 pack) tomato sauce
  • 1 -1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 pc Knorr Pork Cube
  • 1 pc medium sized potato, cut into cubes
  • 1 pc medium sized carrot, cut into cubes
  • 1 pc red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 pc green bell pepper, sliced ground black pepper, to taste

☆ Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Heat pan over medium heat, add oil, and sauté onions and garlic.
  2. Step 2: Add pork and cook until slightly brown.
  3. Step 3: Pour in tomato sauce, water, sugar, and Knorr Pork Broth Cube. Simmer until pork is tender.
  4. Step 4: Add potatoes and carrots and cook until tender.
  5. Step 5: Add bell peppers and season with black pepper. Simmer for 2 minutes and enjoy!

⋆ Bistek Tagalog is a Filipino dish made with marinated beef, soy sauce, calamansi, and onions, creating a savory and tangy flavor. ⋆

☆ Ingredients:

  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup lime juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce (Optional)
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound beef steak, sliced 1/4-inch thick
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided, or as needed
  • 4 potatoes, cut into wedges (Optional)
  • 1 large onion, cut into rings
  • ⅛ cup water (Optional)

☆ Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Sauté garlic and onion in a pot.
  2. Step 2: Add chicken and brown it.
  3. Step 3: Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, water, and add peppercorns and bay leaves.
  4. Step 4: Simmer for 30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  5. Step 5: Adjust seasoning and serve with rice.

☆ Ingredients:

  • 250g pancit bihon (rice noodles)
  • 200g chicken or pork (sliced)
  • cup mixed vegetables (carrots, cabbage, beans)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • Salt & pepper to taste

☆ Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Sauté garlic and onion in a pan.
  2. Step 2: Add meat and cook until browned.
  3. Step 3: Pour in chicken broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
  4. Step 4: Add noodles and mix well until softened.
  5. Step 5: Toss in vegetables and cook for a few more minutes.
  6. Step 6: Season and serve with calamansi.

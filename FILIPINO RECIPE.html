☆ Bistek Tagalog

☆ Kaldereta

Kaldereta is a hearty Filipino stew made with tender beef or goat, slow-cooked in a tomato sauce with potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, and liver spread. It’s flavorful and slightly spicy, often served with rice.

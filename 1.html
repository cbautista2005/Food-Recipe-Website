☆ Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water

☆ Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Marinate chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves for 30 minutes.
  2. Step 2: In a pan, cook the chicken until tender.
  3. Step 3: Add onions, peppercorns, and water, and simmer.
  4. Step 4: Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper, then serve.
